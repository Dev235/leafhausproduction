From RM1 to The World

It started with a simple idea: selling hot Masala Tea for RM1 to students at Multimedia University to fund an MBA. But the Malaysian weather had other plans.

Founder Gisnervern Arikrishnan realized students wanted it COLD. After 2 years of R&D and 16 different formulations, the Leafhaus Canned Masala Tea was born. It preserves the authentic "mamak" kick in a convenient, sterilised can.
