The Leafhaus Story

Leafhaus is a Malaysian-born brand built on three things:

Heritage • Flavour • Convenience

We grew up with masala tea and turmeric milk as comfort drinks after a long day, during deep conversations, after meals with family. As life got busier, we didn't always have time to boil water, crush spices, and stand over the stove.

So we asked ourselves... The Big Questions:

What if you could get that same warmth and authentic flavour... in a can?

What if it was convenient and still respectful of Muslim dietary needs?

What if masala tea could sit beside your favourite sodas in the chiller section?

Leafhaus is our answer.

A bridge between tradition and modern life, packed into every can.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are Leafhaus drinks halal certified?
Yes. Leafhaus drinks are officially halal certified, so you can enjoy our masala tea and turmeric milk with full peace of mind.
What flavours do you have now?
Right now we have Original Masala Tea and Turmeric Milk available. Green Matcha Masala Tea is coming soon. But all 3 are open for Pre Order.
Are these health drinks or medicine?
Leafhaus drinks are inspired by traditional recipes that use spices for digestion, healing and everyday comfort, but they are not medicine. Think of them as halal friendly wellness-style drinks in a can, not a replacement for medical treatment.
How do I order?
Easy. Just WhatsApp us to Pre-order Original Masala Tea or Turmeric Milk, or join the waitlist for Green Matcha Masala Tea.
Do you offer bulk or outlet supply?
Yes. We supply to cafes, shops, resellers and distributors. You can contact our B2B WhatsApp or email us for pricing and details.
