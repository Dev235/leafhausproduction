The Leafhaus Story

From Home Idea to Sold-Out Favourite

It started with a simple question from an MMU student: "Why can't masala tea be as convenient as any canned drink, but still taste like home?"

Our first batch sold out faster than we imagined. Featured on Bernama, Astro Ulagam, and KL Foodie, Leafhaus is bridging tradition and modern life. We crafted this for the morning rush, the late-night study sessions, and the mamak catch-ups.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are Leafhaus drinks halal certified?
Yes. Leafhaus drinks are officially halal certified, so you can enjoy our masala tea and turmeric milk with full peace of mind.
What flavours do you have now?
Right now we have Original Masala Tea and Turmeric Milk available. Green Matcha Masala Tea is coming soon. All 3 are open for Pre-Order.
Are these health drinks or medicine?
Leafhaus drinks are inspired by traditional recipes that use spices for digestion, healing and everyday comfort, but they are not medicine. Think of them as halal-friendly wellness-style drinks.
How do I order?
Easy. Just WhatsApp us to Pre-order Original Masala Tea or Turmeric Milk, or join the waitlist for Green Matcha Masala Tea.
